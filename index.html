About Us

Turtles Society is a collection of 2,222 high-quality and algorithmically generated Turtles in the ocean of Solana.

What Sets It Apart From Other Collections?

We aim to create long-term value for our holders and that’s our #1 priority because we believe it is the best way to build a strong and great community.

We will Airdrop 40% of the Royalties generated every two weeks back to the holders and the remaining 20% will be used to Sweep the Floor. This will discourage paperhands from investing in the project since a portion of the sales is going back to the holder’s wallets.

Roadmap

  • Step 1

    Marketing and launch of mini games to host competitions and keep the community engaged

  • Step 2

    Launch Of 2,222 Turtles To The Community

  • Step 3

    Listing on NFT Marketplaces including Magic Eden and others after Sold Out

  • Step 4

    Setup DAO & Treasury Wallet

  • Step 5

    SOL Airdrop every 2 weeks from 40% Royalties, discouraging paper hands from investing in this project because a portion of the proceeds will go into the wallets of NFT holders.

  • Step 6

    Floor Sweeps, with the other 20% coming from Royalties, to provide even more value to members and keep prices stable.

  • Step 7

    Every NFT is a ticket to a monthly 5 SOL Giveaway, which will take place in the first week of each month.

  • Step 8

    Acquire a plot for holders in Sandbox, build it, and host activities on it. as soon as possible.

  • Step 9

    We intend to launch a Membership Card in the future, which will give members access to exclusive benefits. All of this is part of Roadmap 2.0.
